Air Pollution Monitoring

PythonRaspberry PiIoTSensors

Overview

A real-time IoT-based air quality monitoring system that continuously reads atmospheric gas levels using dedicated sensors. When a dangerous gas concentration reaches or exceeds the safe threshold, the system immediately triggers an alert — sending SMS notifications directly to government officials and safety authorities via a GSM module, and simultaneously pushing live readings to the Blynk mobile app for remote monitoring.

Hardware

  • MQ Gas Sensors Detect harmful gases like CO, CO₂, LPG, Smoke, and NH₃ in the atmosphere in real time.
  • GSM Module Sends automated SMS alerts to pre-configured emergency contacts when thresholds are breached.
  • Raspberry Pi Central processing unit that runs the Python monitoring script and reads sensor data.

Driver Drowsiness

PythonOpenCVComputer VisionDlib

Overview

A computer vision-based safety system that continuously monitors a driver's face in real-time. By tracking facial landmarks and calculating the Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR), the system accurately detects signs of microsleep or severe fatigue, instantly triggering an alarm to wake the driver and prevent potential accidents.

Core Technology

  • Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) A geometric calculation based on facial landmarks to determine eye closure duration and frequency.
  • Real-time Alerting Native integration with audio systems to provide immediate wake-up calls during fatigue detection.

System Architecture

1. Face Tracking

Continuous video stream processing to identify facial orientation and key landmark points.

2. Fatigue Analysis

Mathematical analysis of eyelid movement patterns to distinguish between normal blinking and drowsiness.

3. Safety Response

Instant visual and auditory alerts triggered within 1.5 seconds of detected eye closure.
